Key highlights of the institution include:

Academic Programs:
Offers PU streams in Science and Commerce, along with degree courses in Computers, Business Management, and Administration.

Campus Facilities:
Provides a highly equipped, eco-friendly campus with modern amenities, including hostel facilities, CCTV surveillance, an extensive library, IT infrastructure, and a sports and gym complex.

Holistic Development:
Stresses overall student growth through extracurricular activities, including NCC, NSS, and specialized coaching for competitive exams like K-CET and NEET.
