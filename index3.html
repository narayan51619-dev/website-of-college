Key Academic Programs

The college is well-known for its undergraduate curriculum designed to make students competitive in the modern corporate and IT sectors.

Primary courses include:Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com): Focuses on core subjects like financial accounting, business economics, and corporate management.

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA): A specialized undergraduate degree centered on software development, computer science, and IT skills.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA): Covers management, administration, and organizational leadership.
